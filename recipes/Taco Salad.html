Taco Salad

A delicious image of a taco salad, yum!

Taco salad is a great dish, that orginiated in Texas during the 1960's. Allowing for easier consumption compared to its messy counter part the crunchy taco. The recipe is very similar to the standard taco in which you have the beef which you mix with beans to add a very tasty flavor. You have the dressing, seasonings, chips for an extra crunch, lettuce, and the toppings like cheese, tomatoes, and salsa.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Beans
  • French Salad Dressing
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Chips
  • Lettuce
  • Toppings: Cheese, tomatoes, salsa, sour cream, pico de gallo, corn, jalapenos.

Steps

  1. Cook beef, then stir in the beans, dressing, and seasoning.
  2. Place crushed chips in a bowl, then top with lettuce, cheese, and tomatoes.
  3. Mix in the meat mixture.
  4. Finish the taco salad bowls with sour cream and salsa.
